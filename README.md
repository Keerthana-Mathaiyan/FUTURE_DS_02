
# Facebook Ads Campaign Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Facebook ad campaign data to provide actionable insights into campaign performance. It helps marketers understand how well their ads are performing, identify top-performing segments, and optimize campaigns to maximize ROI.

## Features

- **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**  
  - Total Spend  
  - Total Impressions  
  - Total Clicks  
  - Click-Through Rate (CTR)  
  - Cost per Conversion  
  - Return on Investment (ROI)  

- **Audience Segmentation:**  
  - Breakdown by Age, Gender, and Interests  
  - Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering

- **Ad Performance Analysis:**  
  - Performance by Ad ID

  - Trend analysis over time

- **Optimization Recommendations:**  
  - Identification of high-ROI segments  
  - Suggestions for budget reallocation and creative testing

## Technologies Used

- **Power BI:** For data visualization and dashboard creation  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** For advanced calculations and KPI metrics  
- **Sample Dataset:** Facebook Ads campaign data including impressions, clicks, spend, and conversions

---

## Data Assumptions

- **Value per Approved Conversion:** Assumed at $50 for ROI calculation purposes due to lack of direct revenue data in the dataset.

---

## Getting Started

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Open the Power BI report file (`.pbix`) with Power BI Desktop.
3. Connect the report to your dataset or use the provided sample data.
4. Explore the interactive dashboard visuals.
5. Use slicers to filter data by demographics or campaign details.

---

## How to Use

- Review overall campaign performance using KPI cards.  
- Analyze audience behavior by filtering with Age, Gender, and Interest slicers.  
- Examine ad effectiveness through CTR and conversion rate visuals.  
- Identify budget and targeting optimization opportunities using the funnel and trend charts.

---

## Insights & Recommendations

- The campaign shows a low CTR (~0.01%), indicating room for improving ad creatives or targeting.  
- Cost per Conversion (~$33.54) and ROI (~49%) suggest the campaign is moderately profitable but can be optimized further.  
- Older age groups show higher CTR despite lower spend, presenting a potential growth opportunity.  
- Reallocate budget to higher-performing interests and demographics based on ROI analysis.
